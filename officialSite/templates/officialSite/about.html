My Biography and Experience

Attractive Pixel Inc. was founded in August, 2016 by Samson Samuels, a Computer Science & Education graduate of University of Nigeria, Nsukka. He is someone who is crazily interested in computers and technology and has vowed to be part of the future software developers in which has led him into programming and web developing.

Growing up, Samson was told a story by his parent that in his early age, he hates photographs because the sudden flashlights do scare him alot, so whenever it happens he wants to be photographed, he has to be decieved with chocolates so he can stand still and stable to take photos.

During his university days, while he was pondering, he asked himself on what to embark on careerly, then, his inner-self asked him this question, "WHAT HAVE YOU FOUND YOUSELF DOING SINCE THESE YEARS ?". While searching his PC, he came to realize that he has so many photos of himself, friends, family, people around him, natures and the likes. Nothing less than 5,000 pictures he's taken consciously or unconsciously. This really ignited and boosted his passion for photography, then he realized how important a picture is in one's life.

He got so many motivating words from his dad who he looked up to as a mentor because that was his dad's profession for more than 20 years when he was in his youth days in which he won so many awards.

Samson said, "I have big dreams about photography, I wanna be part of the people who will take photography to a level that has never been imagined nor comprehended. I believe in myself that I can make it".

Photographs is not just about printed images on a hard paper or whatsoever, it's far beyond that!!!. Photographs brings life, speaks life, reminds you of the beautiful moments you've had, it reminds you of who you've been and who you are.

"If you can dream it, gather useful knowledge about it, pray about it, then you can achieve it." - Samson Samuels
